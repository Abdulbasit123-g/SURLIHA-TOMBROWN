What is Surliha Tombrown?

Tombrown is a traditional, highly nutritious porridge flour widely celebrated for its health benefits. It is crafted from a precise, balanced combination of five local powerhouse ingredients:

Corn (Maize): Promotes digestion and provides healthy carbohydrates for active energy.
Millet: Mineral-rich grain providing phosphorus, magnesium, and essential antioxidants.
Guinea Corn (Sorghum): High in dietary fiber, iron, and loaded with essential vitamins.
Groundnuts (Peanuts): Delivers healthy plant fats and proteins for brain development and muscle mass.
Soybeans: Exceptionally high in protein, supplying all essential amino acids.

Step-by-Step Cooking Guide

How to Prepare Tombrown

Follow these simple steps to prepare a smooth, delicious, and lump-free bowl of Tombrown in less than 10 minutes.

Portion Calculator for Cooking

Adjust servings to update powder & water measurements automatically

Servings (People)
1

Mix the Smooth Paste Done

Scoop 3 to 4 tablespoons of **Surliha Tombrown** flour into a small mixing bowl. Pour a small amount of cold water into the bowl and stir thoroughly. Keep stirring until the mixture turns into a completely smooth, consistent liquid paste with zero lumps.

2

Pour into Boiling Water Done

Boil approximately 1.5 to 2 cups of clean water in a pot. Once fully boiling, reduce the heat to low. Slowly pour the smooth Tombrown paste into the boiling water while stirring continuously and vigorously with a wooden spoon or whisk.

3

Cook & Thickens Done

Leave the pot on low-to-medium heat. Let it simmer for **5 to 10 minutes**. Continue to stir consistently to prevent the porridge from sticking to the bottom, burning, or forming clumps. The porridge will gradually thicken and take on a glossy, cooked appearance.

4

Serve, Sweeten & Enjoy! Done

Turn off the heat. Scoop the warm porridge into a serving bowl. (Saying Bismillah is a wonderful way to bring blessings to your meal!). Add your favorite type of milk. Since it has no added sugar, you may **add a small portion of sugar**, honey, or date syrup to add some sweetness to your taste, top with fruits, and enjoy!

How to Make Tombrown Snack Balls

In the flyer, you can see a bowl of round, delicious golden-brown snack balls. These are highly nutritious **Tombrown Energy Bites**! They make the perfect quick snack for school lunchboxes, workouts, or travel. Here is the best way to produce them:

1
Blend the Base: Mix 1 cup of **Surliha Tombrown** flour, 1/2 cup of creamy peanut butter, and 3 tablespoons of raw honey or date syrup in a clean mixing bowl.
2
Bind the Dough: Stir thoroughly. If the mixture is too dry to bind, add 1 to 2 tablespoons of warm milk (or water) until it forms a soft, thick cookie-dough consistency.
3
Roll into Balls: Scoop small spoonfuls of the mixture and roll them firmly between your palms to form smooth, round balls (about the size of golf balls).
4
Set & Enjoy: Roll the balls in toasted coconut flakes or sesame seeds if desired. Chill in the refrigerator for 20 minutes to set. Enjoy this pure grain snack!
